WHY OWN A FRIGGIN EGG ?

Owning a FrigginEgg not only grants you access to our foodie NFT club, you’ll gain access to our in-house Token, Royalties and much more! Together we’ll explore how food will be part of the Metaverse. The journey will start with our FrigginEggs, join us for the ride!

Foodies Club

Gain access to the first NFT project exploring the possibilities of Food & NFTs! We’ll be hosting regular cooking contests. Dish makers will be a rewarded with royalties from NFT sales. Are you are a foodie but don’t have any cooking experience? That’s alright, welcome to join the foodie ride!

Royalties Club

If you happen to get FrigginEgg with a special trait (to be announced) you will have access to the royalties club! Half of the royalties from every secondary sale will be shared among owners of the special NFT equally. It means you will earn passive income simply by holding that NFT in your wallet. How cool is that?!

Community Treasury

After soldout, the Community Treasury will be funded with 20eth. This treasury will be used for the benefit of the community. Whether it be purchasing floor priced FrigginEggs for giveaways, buying merch, donating to an organization or any other initiatives! The choice will be made by the community.

What is $YOLK ?

YIELD

Every FrigginEgg yields 10 $YOLK a day, to start the generation then it will accumulate over time and can be claimed by the owner anytime through our token’s smart contract.

MINT DISHES

Burn $YOLK to mint 3D dishes as NFTs straight from our Friggin Kitchen!

CUSTOMIZE

Got lore for your FrigginEgg? You will be able to create it for your own FrigginEgg! Oh and you can also name your FrigginEgg if you wish to do so!

1 FrigginEgg = 10 $YOLk / day

$YOLK is the utility token that fuels the FrigginEgg ecosystem on the blockchain. The mission of FrigginEggs extends further than just a 2D PFP, or a 3D dish. It's about community, utility, and of course food! Each FrigginEgg owner is able to claim 10 $YOLK tokens per day for the next xxx years. Which means FrigginEggs will produce a total of 28,386,050 $YOLK tokens per year, Now that’s a lot of $YOLK! $YOLK is generated passively can be claimed anytime on our website. As we grow as a community more utility will be added down the road. It is NOT an investment and has NO economic value.

The Friggin Kitchen

We heard you got hungry and have plenty of $YOLK tokens. Wonder what else you can do with them? Welcome to the Friggin Kitchen! Its the place where you’ll be able to use $YOLK tokens and mint 3D-dishes as NFTs! These will be the first edible NFTs created by the help of our foodie community itself. They will gain value over time

MADE BY THE COMMUNITY

The dishes will be ERC-1155 NFTs, and collectible 3D assets made entirely by the help of our foodie community. We plan on hosting fun cooking events and competitions. Winners will have their dish forever stored on the ethereum blockchain as NFTs collectibles. As reward the dish makers will get royalties from every sale of the NFT, earning them passive income for life! Who would have thought being a foodie could earn you passive income? Together let’s make it happen!
