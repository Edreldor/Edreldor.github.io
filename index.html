ABOUT US

Hello virtual foodies! Welcome to the FrigginEggs Family! We are the first NFT project trying to explore how food can be linked with NFTs. While most NFT projects out there have a sci-fi/futuristic theme, we wanted to go for a more down-to-earth theme that can actually have an impact on our health, which we feel is lacking in the NFT community right now. Our team have all spent hours playing HarvestMoon (yes on that old gameboy lol) while we were young and absolutely loved it. So we wanted to bring some of these memories back while still embracing the NFT space, and giving something valuable back to the community. That is how FrigginEggs was born!

OUR COLLECTION

Our NFT collectible is based on an egg, the FrigginEgg Collection. There will be 9,999 NFTs (100 reserved for events and giveaways) and are randomly generated with cool accessories. Every FrigginEgg is rare but some have unique items and special traits that make them super rare! We’ve spent a long time perfecting the rarity system. Some even give you passive income based on secondary sales!

OUR GOAL

We want to build a healthier NFT community and we believe that food is the answer. Our goal is to encourage cooking and be more concious on our food choices. Why you may ask? Well the answer is very simple: We are what we eat and we believe that cooking plays a big part of being healthy, besides its a lot of fun, brings so much satisfaction and save you money too! Our health should always be the No.1 priority no matter if you are an NFT whale or have reached financial freedom. We’d like to build a healthy and fun community to learn and share from each other, no matter the cooking experience.

SPOILER ALERT! Expect lots of fun challenges to come!!

WHY OWN A FRIGGIN EGG ?

If owning a dope piece of art wasn't enough, there are other benefits that come with being a FrigginEgg owner. We have a few utilities and clubs which you will automatically be a part of just by owning an NFT from the collection.

Cooking Club & Hall of fame

Gain access to the first project exploring the possibilities of Food & NFTs! We’ll be hosting regular cooking contests and the best dishes will be minted in our Hall-Of-Fame collection. The chefs will be a rewarded half of the proceeds when it is sold. Dont have any cooking experience? That’s alright, hopefully we’ll inspire you to start cooking! Together we’ll share tips and tricks that can be applied right away.

Own a land on the Friggin Farm

Our farm is pretty huge and in the near future we plan to give every FrigginEgg holder a piece of land as an NFT. We are still brainstorming on the idea and your feedback is important. There are many possibilities and we’d like to give something that is valuable to the community. Should we sow some seeds? Hatch some eggs? Breed some animals? Do food workshops? Join the conversation and together we’ll try to make it happen!

Foodies Club

Congratulations! Those who own an NFT from the FrigginEgg NFT collection are officially considered a Friggin Foodie! You will have access to the private discord channel where your voice will be heard by the team and you will have voting rights on the project’s development and future. Together let’s build something fun and food related.

Royalties Club

If you happen to get FrigginEgg with a special trait (to be announced) you will have access to the royalties club! Half of the royalties from every secondary sale will be shared among owners of the special NFT equally. It means you will earn passive income simply by holding that NFT in your wallet. How cool is that?!

Community Treasury

After sales are over a percentage will be sent to the Community Treasury. NFT holders will be able to vote on how to spend the funds, whether it be purchasing floor priced FrigginEggs from the opensea for giveaways, producing content, buying merch, donating to an organization or any other initiatives! The choice will be made entirely by the community.

RARITY

All 3,000 FrigginEggs are algorithmically generated crypto collectible NFTs that have been generated using more than 100 hand drawn items, each is unique and range in rarity. Each Friggin egg has a base score (based on its items) and the background color represents the rarity tier. Background patterns do not contribute to the total score but will only appear on 30% of all eggs, some rarer than others and only the rarest pattern give you access to the Royalties Club
